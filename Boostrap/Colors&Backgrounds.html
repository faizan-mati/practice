  --bs-success: #198754;

13. Best Practices & Tips

✓ Do:
  • • Use semantic colors (success for positive actions)
  • • Maintain sufficient contrast for accessibility
  • • Use subtle variants for backgrounds
  • • Combine with appropriate text colors
  • • Use opacity for layered effects
✗ Avoid:
  • • Overusing bright colors
  • • Poor color contrast combinations
  • • Using colors without semantic meaning
  • • Mixing too many different color schemes
  • • Ignoring color accessibility guidelines
